Software Engineering — minimum requirements

For Tech Lead, Senior, Mid-level, Junior, and Intern roles. Inspired by practices at Ebanx, Google, Meta, and Ericsson.

  • Bachelor's degree in CS, Engineering, or related field or equivalent practical experience
  • Proficiency in at least one language (e.g. Python, Java, C/C++, Go, JavaScript/TypeScript)
  • Version control (Git) and collaborative workflows
  • Understanding of data structures, algorithms, and software design
  • Interest in distributed systems, concurrency, and persistence
  • Good communication and problem-solving skills
  • English proficiency (written and spoken) for international collaboration
