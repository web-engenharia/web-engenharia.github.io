Privacy policy

Minimal version for static site.

This page describes, in general terms, the processing of personal data associated with the use of the Web-Engenharia website and the sending of messages through the contact form (visitor forwarding via WhatsApp).

The contact form requests name, email, optionally company and message. This data is sent only when the visitor confirms sending, opening WhatsApp with the filled message; Web-Engenharia does not store this information on its own server in this static version of the site.

Purpose

To respond to contact requests, qualify business demands, and maintain communication related to the subject of the message.

Legal basis

Execution of pre-contractual measures at the data subject's request and legitimate interest in responding to requests, as applicable — confirm with your organization's DPO or legal advisor.

Data subject rights

Under GDPR/LGPD, you may request confirmation of processing, access, correction, anonymization, deletion, or information about sharing. Send requests to contact@web-engenharia.com.

Cookies and third parties

This landing may load fonts and scripts from CDNs (e.g., Google Fonts, Tailwind). Consult these vendors' policies. We do not use our own analytics cookies in this minimal version, except for future inclusion documented here.

This policy may be changed. The date of the last revision should be indicated below when publishing to production.
