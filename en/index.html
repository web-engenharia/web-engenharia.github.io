Engagement models

Flexibility for your context: hourly rate, fixed price, retainer, shared success, and special cases.

Hourly rate (HT)

Billing per hour worked. Time & Materials, ideal for variable scope and exploratory sprints.

Ideal for: projects with unpredictable demand or discovery phases.

Fixed price

Fixed fee with defined scope and schedule. Predictability for client and consultant.

Ideal for: well-defined scope and low requirement volatility.

Subscription (Retainer)

PMO as a Service or ongoing consulting. Recurring availability and predictable revenue.

Ideal for: continuous governance, evolving integrations, or recurring support.

Success fee

Compensation tied to goals. Success fee when pre-defined objectives are achieved.

Ideal for: projects with measurable ROI and win-win alignment.

Vesting (startups)

Progressive equity participation. Equity with cliff and calendar for long-term partnerships.

Ideal for: early-stage startups, CTOs, and strategic advisors.

Pro-bono

Voluntary or subsidized work for social impact, ESG causes, and community capacity building.

Ideal for: NGOs, impact initiatives, and strategic cause portfolios.

Hybrid

Combination of models: retainer + success fee, hourly + vesting, according to maturity and profile.

Ideal for: commercial flexibility and risk alignment between parties.

Service catalog

Integrations and API-first architecture in four pillars, plus project management and IT governance consulting — each block translates technical capability into business results.

Integrations & Architecture

Discovery & design

System and contract mapping; non-functional requirements (latency, idempotency, resilience); patterns (API gateway, messaging, CDC). Goal: less rework and surprises in production.

Implementation

Connectors, REST APIs and events, orchestration and on-demand iPaaS, ETL and streaming. Goal: delivery that scales with the business.

Operation

SLIs/SLOs for integrations, monitoring, runbooks, and incident management across vendors and internal teams. We treat integration as product — not as an isolated script.

Governance

OAuth/OIDC, secrets, mTLS when applicable, living documentation (OpenAPI, event catalog), and audit trail. Security and compliance by design, not as an afterthought.

Project Management

Diagnostics & maturity

Maturity assessment with OPM3 and ITIL. Process and effectiveness audit (not just compliance). Deliverable: Improvement Program with identified gaps and evolution roadmap.

PMO & portfolio management

Project Office implementation (PMO Lite to Full, or Agile). Strategic prioritization, EVM, and initiative selection. Performance metrics, dashboards, and PMO as a Service.

ITSM & service catalog

Incident, change, problem, and asset management (CMDB). Platform implementation (ServiceNow, Jira SM, GLPi), self-service portals, and automated approval workflows.

Methodologies & capital projects

Waterfall, Scrum, Kanban, and hybrid models per context. EVM, CAPEX/OPEX management, procurement and contracts. Engineering and infrastructure projects with industrial risk management.
