What it is

A distributed, immutable double-entry accounting engine built for teams that need reliable financial close at scale.

The problem

Companies that grow fast — marketplaces, fintechs — often lose control of the close due to database inconsistencies and lack of per-transaction tracing.

The value

Real-time audit: every cent is tracked by isolated Elixir processes, with an immutable trail so balances cannot change without a recorded, explainable event.
