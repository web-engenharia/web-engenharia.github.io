Common challenges

Stockouts, shrink, and POS slowdowns typically show up when integration is fragile and lacks governance.

Stockouts & synchronization

Selling online what’s no longer on the shelf creates frustration. Without real-time sync, omnichannel operations suffer.

Loss prevention

Perishables expiring before being sold or priced correctly become invisible—yet preventable—loss.

Infrastructure bottlenecks

On holidays and campaigns, POS slows down, queues grow, and payment processing fails under high concurrency.

Solutions

Four technical fronts to connect store operations, e-commerce, and back-office with resilience, low latency, and governance.

High concurrency & real-time

Millisecond-level stock sync across physical stores, e-commerce, and delivery apps; resilient POS and payment processing for peaks like Black Friday.

AI & data

Semantic search in e-commerce to suggest the right items and predictive dynamic pricing for perishables nearing expiry, reducing waste.

Event-driven monitoring

Cold-chain IoT with immediate maintenance alerts and automated shelf stockout notifications when critical thresholds are reached.

B2B platforms

Supplier portals for delivery scheduling and invoice management; fleet management and route optimization to meet SLAs and reduce fuel cost.
