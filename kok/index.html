    Karar model

    Tuje vatavaran khatir lagpod: tantlean ginn, thollavnn ginn, retainer, vattam jait, ani vishesh pramann.

    Tantlean ginn (HT)

    Kaam zalem tantlean billing. Time & Materials, vadhullam sop ani sodvonit sprint khatir ideal.

    Ideal: apollovnn magneachem va sodvonit taranchim proiekt khatir.

    Thollavnn ginn

    Spoxt sop ani kollavnn asat thollavnn toch. Grahak ani consultant khatir pollovnn.

    Ideal: borem spoxt sop ani nemsam gorjevoll adharn.

    Nimann (Retainer)

    PMO Service mhonn va sootadhar consultadoria. Nimann upalobdhta ani pollovnn ichir.

    Ideal: sootadhar vaangddop, vaddtaleo integrasaun, va nimann sahaya.

    Jait tochir

    Uddesh borear toch. Pelelean spoxt uddesh sodle zalear jait tochir.

    Ideal: ROI mapan zata tyo proiekt ani donui bhaganchi mell kor.

    Vesting (startups)

    Kramik bhagdar. Lambkam bhagidar khatir cliff ani kalender asat equity.

    Ideal: pelelea taranchim startups, CTOs, ani stratejik sallagar khatir.

    Nishulb

    Somajik porinam, ESG karan ani somudai tokdeari khatir ichchhen kaam va subsidy.

    Ideal: NGOs, porinam chaluim, ani stratejik karan portfolio khatir.

    Mishrit

    Modelanchi mell: retainer + jait tochir, tantlean + vesting, paripakvota ani profile pormonnem.

    Ideal: vepar lagpod ani donui bhaganchea modim riskechi mell.

    Seva sutra

    Chari stambanchim integrasaun ani API-first vasturchna, ani projet vaangddop ani IT adhikar consultadoria — har block technical tokdi vepar phollant badalta.

    Integrasaun ani vasturchna

    Sodvonit ani rochna

    System ani karar map; non-functional gorje (latency, idempotency, resilience); paddhati (API gateway, messaging, CDC). Uddesh: production-an nemsam punr-kaam ani surpriz.

    Amal

    Connectors, REST APIs ani events, orchestration ani on-demand iPaaS, ETL ani streaming. Uddesh: vepar borabor mellok denna.

    Chalovnni

    Integrasaunk SLIs/SLOs, monitoring, runbooks, ani vendor ani bhitorlea tolleanchim incident vaangddop. Ami integrasaun upkarn mhonn mandtam — isolated script mhonn nant.

    Adhikar

    OAuth/OIDC, gupt, mTLS zaka lagta, jivit dokumentason (OpenAPI, event sutra), ani audit trail. Rochnent suraksha ani compliance, uprant mhonn nant.

    Projet vaangddop

    Nidan ani paripakvota

    OPM3 ani ITIL-an paripakvota mulyankan. Paddhati ani prabhav audit (compliance ekloch nant). Meldunk: mellop ani vaddop rochana asat sudharonn karyakram.

    PMO ani portfolio vaangddop

    Projet Office amal (PMO Lite te Full, va Agile). Stratejik prioritization, EVM, ani chaluim nivadd. Prabhav mapan, dashboards, ani PMO Service mhonn.

    ITSM ani seva sutra

    Incident, badall, samasya ani asset vaangddop (CMDB). Platform amal (ServiceNow, Jira SM, GLPi), swayam-seva portale, ani automated mannvon workflow.

    Paddhati ani punji proiekt

    Waterfall, Scrum, Kanban ani vatavaran pormonnem mishrit model. EVM, CAPEX/OPEX vaangddop, khareddi ani karar. Engineer ani infrastructure proiekt industrial risk vaangddop borabor.

    Computer drushtikon

    Data vijnan ani mulyankan

    Sodanch vicharlele prashna

    Oi. Ami data minimization, lagta zalear processing karar, audit trail ani prugtt niyontron borabor kaam kortam — tuje legal tolle ani DPO borabor sodeak mell.
    Sop, legacy API paripakvota ani POC gorjevar adar. Sodvonit uprant ami avk borabor yojna ditam — focused MVPs khatir satolle pasun vhoddlea karyakram khatir trimestram meren.
    Donui: integrasaun tara, BFF portale, automation ani existing systems vaddop — spoxt karar ani sootadhar chalovnni lokx sodeak.
    Zata-zata IT va vasturchna sponsor, vepar niyaman lagim upkarn. Go-live vendor var adar zalear ami riti ani javabdar melltat, teakhatir cutover eklo linkachea hathan zat na.
    Ami upalobd surfaces map kortam — files, database, queues, scheduled exports, va UI automation gorjech zalear — ani surakshit integrasaun tara rochta. Zaka lagta, kramik strangling chodd spoxt karar khatir.
    Zaitir duri, mell kelolea vellar synchronous riti. Sthanak bhet ani workshop gorje ani sop mell pormonnem upalobd.
    Projet taran pormonnem (sodvonit, amal, chalovnni), sootadhar vaddop khatir retainer, va mell. Pelelem sodvonit vhoddlea commitment adim spoxt ani avk yojna dena. Amchea karar model polloo.
